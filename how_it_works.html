How VitalCheck Works for You

We know that finding the right supplements can be confusing. With so many options and conflicting advice, it's hard to know what's right for your body. That's why we created VitalCheck - to take the guesswork out of supplements and give you personalized recommendations based on your unique needs.

The Science Behind Your Recommendations

Our system analyzes hundreds of scientific studies to match supplements to your specific situation:

  • Your Age & Gender: Nutritional needs change throughout life - what works at 25 might not be right at 55
  • Allergies & Sensitivities: We carefully filter out anything you might react to
  • Your Health Goals: Whether you're looking for more energy, better sleep, or joint support
  • Potential Interactions: We consider how supplements might work together
